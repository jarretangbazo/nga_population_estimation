# Nigeria Population Estimates
 Estimating the population of Local Government Areas (LGAs)/Local Council Development Areas (LCDAs), and Federal Constituencies in Nigeria using estimates based on the 2006 census, supplemented with GIS data 

## Data Sources
Nigeria Subnational Population Statistics - UNFPA
https://data.humdata.org/dataset/cod-ps-nga
- `nga_admpop_2020.xlsx`: contains 2020 population estimates at the ADM-2 (Local Government Area) level 
- `nga_codps_qa_2023.pdf`: provides 2022 intrinsic population growth rates (PGR) at the ADM-0 (National) and ADM-1 (State) level for Male, Female, and Both 
  - NOTE: PGR missing for Nasarawa State. Supplemented with Annual Population Change Rate from CityPopulation.de (see below)
  - PGR are entered in the spreadsheet: `NGA_PGR_2022.xlsx`

CityPopulation.de
State and Local Government Area (LGA) population according to census results and latest population projections.
